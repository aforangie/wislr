High school students are social (awkward at times) and over 60% have smartphones.

To meet this opportunity, wislr is creating the world's first mobile network exclusively for high school students (even the awkward ones). We connect them to their two primary networks friends and classmates in a fun and unique way, with no risk of detention!

We're Hiring!

We are looking for a Lead Ninja-neers who can not only code, but are excited to learn and participate in all facets of building a young company. We have a laidback (uniform free) environment: discussing new product ideas, designing features, hacking, and disrupting class. Being popular (or even liked) in high school is not a requirement...
